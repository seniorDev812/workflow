WEBSITE COOKIE POLICY

Your personal data is processed by Seen Group (“COMPANY” or “Seen Group”) as the data controller. Protecting the privacy of visitors to the website (www.seengroup.com) is one of our main principles. This Cookie Usage Policy (“GDPR”) explains to all website visitors and users what types of cookies are used and under which conditions.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device or network server by websites you visit through your computer or mobile device.

They are typically used to offer a personalized experience while using the website, improve the services provided, and enhance your experience. They can also contribute to ease of use while navigating the site. If you prefer not to use cookies, you can delete or block cookies from your browser settings. However, we would like to remind you that this may affect your use of our website. Unless you change your cookie settings from your browser, we will assume you accept the use of cookies on this site.

1. WHAT TYPES OF DATA ARE PROCESSED IN COOKIES?

Cookies on websites collect data about your browsing and usage preferences on the device you visit the site with, depending on their type. These data include information about the pages you access, services and products you view, your preferred language option, and other preferences.

2. WHAT ARE COOKIES AND WHAT ARE THEIR PURPOSES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device or network server via browsers by the websites you visit. These small text files, which include your preferred language and other settings, help remember your preferences on subsequent visits and assist in improving our services to enhance your site experience. This allows for a better and more personalized user experience during your next visit.

The main purposes of using cookies on our website are as follows:

  • Improve the services provided to you by increasing the functionality and performance of the website,
  • Improve the website and offer new features through the website, and personalize the features according to your preferences;
  • Ensure the legal and commercial security of the website, as well as prevent fraudulent transactions on the site;
  • Fulfill the legal and contractual obligations arising from laws such as the Law on Regulating Publications on the Internet and the Regulation on the Procedures and Principles regarding Publications on the Internet.

3. TYPES OF COOKIES USED ON OUR WEBSITE

3.1. Session Cookies
Session cookies are used to ensure the proper functioning of the website during your visit. They help ensure the security and continuity of your visit. Session cookies are temporary cookies and are deleted when you close your browser or revisit the website, as they are not permanent.

3.2. Persistent Cookies
These cookies are used to remember your preferences and are stored on your device via browsers. Persistent cookies remain stored even after you close your browser or restart your computer. These cookies are stored in your browser’s subfolders until they are deleted from your browser settings.

Some types of persistent cookies may be used to offer you personalized recommendations based on your purpose of use of the website.

Persistent cookies check whether a cookie has been created by our website when you revisit it with the same device, and if so, it is understood that you have visited the site before. The content to be delivered to you will be determined accordingly, providing you with better service.

3.3. Necessary/Technical Cookies
These are necessary cookies for the website to function properly. Their purpose is to provide essential services by ensuring the proper functioning of the website. For example, they allow access to the secure sections of the website, the use of features, and navigation.

3.4. Analytical Cookies
These cookies collect information about how the site is used, the frequency and number of visits, and show how visitors reached the site. The purpose of using these cookies is to improve the site’s functionality and increase performance, as well as to determine the general trends. They do not contain data that can identify visitors, such as the number of error messages displayed or the most visited pages.

3.5. Functional Cookies
These cookies remember the choices made by visitors on the site and recall them on the next visit. Their purpose is to provide ease of use for visitors. For example, they prevent the site user from having to re-enter their password on each page visited.

3.6. Targeting/Advertising Cookies
These cookies allow the measurement of the effectiveness of the advertisements shown and the calculation of how many times an advertisement has been viewed. Their purpose is to display personalized advertisements tailored to visitors’ interests.

Likewise, they help identify the interests of visitors based on their browsing and offer appropriate content. For example, they prevent showing the same advertisement to the visitor within a short time frame.

4. HOW TO MANAGE COOKIE PREFERENCES?

To change your preferences regarding the use of cookies, or to block or delete cookies, you only need to change your browser settings.

Many browsers offer you the option to accept or reject cookies, accept only specific types of cookies, or be warned when a website requests to store cookies on your device.

It is also possible to delete previously stored cookies from your browser.

If you disable or reject cookies, you may need to manually adjust some preferences, and some features and services on the website may not work properly because we may not be able to recognize and link your account. You can change your browser settings by clicking on the relevant link in the table below.

The Privacy Policy of the Website is effective as of 26.11.2024. If the entire Policy or certain provisions are updated, the effective date of the Policy will be revised.
